Ernakulam is a town as well as district was formed on April 1, 1958. The district includes Aluva, Kunnathunadu, Kochi, Kanayannur, Paravoor Taluks which was with Thrissur district earlier. Ernakulam is one of the most developed districts in Kerala. It was formed by the two princely states of Travancore and Cochin and the British Malabar. Being commercial hub of Kerala, all the major developments in any sector, it all happens here. Ernakulam is blessed with a wonderful culture and grandeur. It is the fact that Ernakulam is the first district in the whole country to have 100% literacy is a direct indicator of the enviable educational standards of its inhabitants.
